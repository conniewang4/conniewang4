About Us

Abortion for All is a website founded on the basis of fighting for the right to a legal abortion in all 50 states of America. Currently abortion is banned in the following states:

  • Alabama

  • Arizona

  • Arkansas

  • Michigan

  • Mississippi

  • Oklahoma

  • West Virginia

  • Wisconsin

Abortion For All aims to educate not only women that are now put in potential danger and risk of having their lives forcibly changed, but also everyone in America. This is because abortion has a larger and broader effect than many people think. Our goal is to inform the public and take action to do everything we can to support abortion rights.
